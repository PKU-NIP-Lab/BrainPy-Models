1.4 Firing Rate models

Firing Rate models are simpler than reduced models. In these models, each compute unit represents a neuron group, the membrane potential variable VVV in single neuron models is replaced by firing rate variable aaa (or rrr or ν\nuν). Here we introduce a canonical firing rate unit.
